About Us

Step into Pink Tea Sta.Maria, where every sip tells a story. We blend passion with every bubble and pearl to craft delightful, wallet-friendly beverages. Embrace our pet-friendly haven where Mufasa, Scar, and Nala wag their tails in greeting. Dive into experiences beyond the cup - from noodle adventures with ramen-making sessions to thrilling arcade games. Whether you seek a quiet spot for study or a lively hangout with friends, our ambiance invites you to linger and create unforgettable moments.

At the heart of Pink Tea's success is our founder's exceptional ability to blend creativity with business expertise. Her visionary leadership has transformed a love for quality tea into a beloved community hub. Through innovative events like ramen-making sessions and engaging arcade games, she fosters an environment where customers not only enjoy great drinks but also feel inspired and welcomed. Her dedication to crafting a unique experience for every visitor ensures that Pink Tea continues to be a cherished gathering place where relationships are nurtured and stories unfold.
